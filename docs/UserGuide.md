### Registration
1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** page.
2. Enter your **Institutional Email** (must end in `@1.ustp.edu.ph`).
3. Select your **Course/Program**.
4. Create a secure password.

### Login
1. Log in via the **Login** page using your registered email and password.
2. Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the **Home Feed**.

## üìù Features & How-to

### Creating a Post
1. Navigate to the **Lost & Found**, **Events**, or **Academic** section.
2. Click the **"Post"** button.
    * **Lost & Found**: Available to **ALL** users.
    * **Announcements/Events**: Available **ONLY** to Organization Heads assigned by a Moderator.
3. Fill in the details (e.g., Title, Description, Images).
4. Submit the post.

### Managing Posts
* You can **edit** or **delete** a post if you are the owner (Lost & Found) or the current Head of the posting organization (Announcements/Events).

### Messaging (Lost & Found)
1. Locate a Lost & Found post you have information about.
2. Click the **‚Ä¢‚Ä¢‚Ä¢** button on the post card.
3. Select **Chat with poster**. A modal will appear where you can draft and send your message.
4. Access your chat history via the **message bubble** icon. Clicking a specific user in the list displays the conversation history.

### Search
* Use the search bar at the top of the Feed, Lost & Found, Events, or Academic sections.
* Enter keywords to match post **Titles**.
* Search is context-aware (Global or specific to the current section).

### Witness Feature
* If you can vouch for a Lost & Found item (e.g., you saw it where the poster claims), you can mark yourself as a **Witness**.
* **Note:** This action adds credibility to the post and **cannot be undone**.

### Saved Posts
* Click the **Bookmark/Save** icon on any post to add it to your **Saved Posts** list for quick reference.

### Moderator Privileges
* Moderators can create, read, edit, and delete organization details.
