### 1. Lost & Found
*   **Purpose**: Community-driven reporting of lost items.
*   **Access**: Open to **ALL** users.
*   **Key Fields**: Item Name, Description, Location, Image, Date.
*   **Special Feature**: **Witnesses**. Other users can confirm the validity of a lost item report.

### 2. Announcements
*   **Purpose**: Official updates from organizations.
*   **Access**: **Organization Heads** and **Moderators** only.
*   **Visibility**: Pinned or highlighted in the main feed.

### 3. Events
*   **Purpose**: Campus activities and schedules.
*   **Access**: **Organization Heads** and **Moderators** only.
*   **Features**: Users can "Save" events to get reminders (future feature).
